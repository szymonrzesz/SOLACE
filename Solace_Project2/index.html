our mission

At Solace we want to bring warmth and comfort to the daily lives of those struggling with mental health, and feel a bit down or stressed. There is a direct correlation between mental health and animals. Animals provide comfort during our times of need, when maybe you’re too worn down to ask for human help. We aim to create a meaningful experience that can lighten the burden of those that need it. With stray cats on the rise, and shelters running out of room, we made it our mission to find new homes for these felines while bringing joy to those who might need it.
